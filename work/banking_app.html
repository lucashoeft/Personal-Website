Banking App

I had the idea to make the design for a banking app that works like Google Inbox. The app recognizes transfers and puts a thumbnail and a detail page to it instead of showing just the numbers. Because it is a banking app and it is focused on numbers, I have chosen gray as the main color because it is less distracting and the app looks more reliable than an app full of colorful gradients.
