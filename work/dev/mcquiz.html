MC Quiz — Contact & Privacy Note

I truly believe that your data should only belong to you. The app stores all your data (for example the settings) locally on your device. No usage data is being collected or shared with third parties.

If you opt in to share analysis data with third party developers in your system settings, the developer of this app will receive consolidated statistics about the number of active users. Furthermore, crash reports may be forwarded to the developer for easier trouble shooting.

When contacting the developer from the app settings, the developer may store your information such as email address and any other information that you provide. The information is used to solve support issues and improve the product.
