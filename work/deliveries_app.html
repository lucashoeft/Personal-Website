Deliveries App

The app is designed minimalistic and the focus is on the key value — tracking the shipping of your parcels. The shipping status is divided in three main stages like the colors. Grey means that the parcel will arrive in longer than a week what usually not happens. The yellow color indicates that the parcel arrives soon and last, green means that the parcel has arrived.
